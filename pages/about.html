About

A small publication about attention.

Quiet Cartography is a fictional editorial project , built as a frontend portfolio demo. The goal is to show design restraint, clear hierarchy, and polished interactions.

Editorial principles

Minimal doesn’t mean empty , it means intentional. A calm layout gives your content room to breathe and your reader room to think.

In a good interface, nothing begs for attention , it earns it.

What’s included

  • Light/dark mode with saved preference
  • JS-rendered blog index + post template
  • Category filtering + search
  • Sticky header with subtle behavior
  • Accessible focus states and semantic markup
